Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of eleven players. It is widely popular in countries like India, Australia, England, and many others. The game involves batting, bowling, and fielding with the objective of scoring more runs than the opposition. Cricket has different formats such as Test matches, One Day Internationals (ODIs), and T20 matches.
